Version 1.1.0

- **Enhanced Private DNS Management:**
  - Multiple DNS hostnames: choose from predefined servers (Cloudflare, AdGuard, Quad9) or add custom entries.
  - Customizable Tile Cycle: select which hostnames (including Off/Auto) appear in the tile cycle.
  - Info Buttons: Learn about the benefits of predefined DNS servers directly within the app.
- **UI Improvement:**
  - The Private DNS settings card is now scrollable for a better user experience with many hostname entries.
